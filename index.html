About Me

Hi! I am Ray. I have an electronics engineering degree and after some life adventures, I ended up being a Sofware Engineer and enjoying it very much. I am currently working in a well-known educational organization and trying hard to provide a good web experience to our customers.

My technical skills are mostly tied to the applications I worked on and I also worked on side projects to explore new tools.

I used Java and JavaScript extensively. I worked on AWS environment in an effort to move our legacy applications into cloud. I developed several microservices with node.js and used most of the AWS components.

Maintaining our legacy Java applications designed with Spring and Struts frameworks was on me and I made major changes in these applications to keep them up and running when the local dependencies were moved to cloud one-by-one.

Automation testing is my strongest suite and I created my own frameworks one with Java/Selenium/JUnit/Cucumber and another with JavaScript/WebDriverIO/Selenium/Chai/Cucumber framework which both were actively used for different applications.

I am currently improving my web technology skills and studying html/css/bootstrap/ajax/jquery.
